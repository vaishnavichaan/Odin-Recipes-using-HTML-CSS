Palak Chicken

If you want to give the classic palak paneer a meaty twist, then this palak chicken is definitely worth a try! It is loaded with essential nutrients and vitamins and makes for a wholesome dinner meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 gms Chicken (Curry Cut)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 4 tbsp Dahi
  • to taste Salt
  • 2 cups Spinach Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Kasuri Methi

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken in salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and 4 tbsp dahi for 30 minutes. Set aside while you prepare the remaining curry ingredients.
  2. One bunch each of coriander leaves, kasuri methi (fenugreek) leaves, four green chilies, and four to five cashew nuts should be added to a blender jar. Set that aside; don't blend it yet because the spinach leaves will be added later.
  3. To prevent the spinach leaves from sticking, heat a pan over medium heat and add just enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan. Add 2 cups of spinach leaves, and cover the pan for about a minute to let the spinach wilt.
  4. Remove the lid after a minute and stir the spinach leaves until they are just wilted. If the spinach leaves are overcooked, they will lose their vibrant green color.

Chicken Gyro

Yogurt-marinated chicken topped on pita bread with tzatziki sauce - this chicken gryo is wholesome, refreshing can be made in just under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 120 gm Chicken thigh
  • 5 gm Thyme
  • 25 gm Paprika powder
  • 5 gm Salt
  • 5 gm Pepper
  • 100 gm Hung curd
  • 25 gm Cucumber

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine marinade ingredients and stir well.
  2. On a cutting board, place a piece of plastic wrap over the chicken thighs and pound until about 1/2 inch 1 cm thick with a meal mallet rolling pin or heavy pan.
  3. In other large bowl or gallon plastic bag, combine chicken thighs and marinade, stirring well to coat. Cover the refrigerate for at least one hour and up to one day
  4. Preheat the oven to 200 degree C. Shred the cucumber. (Make sure to squeeze out any excess liquid.)
  5. In a medium bowl, combine tzatziki ingredients and stir well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 mins.

Chicken Tawa Boti

A quick and easy chicken recipe, this chicken tawa boti only requires stir-frying of all the ingredients on a tawa. It has a masaledar taste and can be enjoyed as an evening snack.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Onions (diced)
  • 250 gm Chicken Breast (diced)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala

Method

  1. Heat oil in a tawa and start by sauting chopped ginger, garlic and green chillies.
  2. Stir fry till the raw smell of the ginger and garlic goes away.
  3. Next, add the diced onion and chicken. Fry well.
  4. Season with salt, garam masala, red chilli powder and turmeric powder. Fry on high flame.

One Pot Chicken Pasta

Diced chicken is saut√©ed in olive oil and topped with tomato puree, cream, parsley, Italian seasoning and cooked in chicken broth. This one pot chicken pasta can be made in
just under 30 minutes and is sure to leave you drooling! Garnish it with parmesan and mozzarella cheese and indulge in its goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 500 gram Chicken (diced in cubes)
  • to taste Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup Onion (diced)
  • 2 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh parsley

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute chicken till it loses moisture. Season it with pepper and salt.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic. Once the onion is translucent, add Italian seasoning, parsley, broth, tomatoes, cream and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add the raw pasta and let the pasta cook in the broth. Add more broth, if needed to cook the boiled pasta.
  4. Garnish the pasta with parmesan cheese and mozzarella cheese and mix it well. The one pot chicken pasta is ready!
