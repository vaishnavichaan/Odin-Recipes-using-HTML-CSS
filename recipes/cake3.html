Kesar Mango Lassi

Everyone likes to drink lassi in the summer season and if the taste of mango i.e. mango is added to your favorite lassi, then imagine how fun that drink will be. That's why today we have brought for you the recipe of Mango Lassi to beat the heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mango Pulp
  • 2 cups Curd
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Saffron soaked in water
  • A pinch of Green Cardamom Powder

Method

  1. Take mango pulp in a blender, add curd to it along with it.
  2. After this, add sugar, cardamom powder, milk and saffron and blend everything well.
  3. Smooth and thick Kesar Mango Lassi is ready.

Sharbat

The refreshing flavour of mint leaves and the sweetness of gur combine in this recipe. This easy to make sharbat will refresh you in an instant. Try it out today.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp Jaggery
  • 4-5 Mint Leaves
  • A pinch of pink salt and jeera powder
  • 1 cup Cold water

Method

  1. In a glass add some jeera powder.
  2. Now take some mint leaves and grind them to take out its juice.
  3. Add the mint juice with a few leaves in a glass.
  4. Then add one tablespoon of gur (jaggery) water in the glass with a hint of pink salt.
  5. Add crushed ice, and chilled water. Mix and enjoy!

kesar and Mix Nuts Thandai

Besides colours and gulaal, thandai is probably the first thing we associate with Holi. So, here we bring you a unique recipe of Thandai made with avocado and mixed nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Fresh Avocado (puree
  • 25 gram Almonds (badam)
  • 25 gram Cashew nuts (kaju)
  • 5 gram White Poppy seeds (khus khus)

Method

  1. Dry roast all the nuts separately.
  2. Soak Cashew nuts, White poppy seeds, Pistachio, Fennel seeds, Black peppercorn and Cardamom seeds powder in 1 cup of milk and keep it aside for2-3 hours. Grind it into a Thandai paste. Add Avocado puree. Mix well.
  3. Heat 1 lit of milk. Add sugar, saffron strands and bring it to a boil.
  4. Mix the Thandai avocado mixture into the milk and stir well. Let it cool down and refrigerate for 2-3 hours to chill.
  5. Once it is chilled, serve in a glass, add chopped pistachios, and dried rose petals on top.
