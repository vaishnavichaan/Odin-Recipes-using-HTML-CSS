Khatta Meetha Dhokla

Give a sweet and tangy twist to the classic dhokla with this khatta meetha dhokla recipe. It is super soft and spongy and is topped with a flavourful tempering. You can have this delicious Gujarati delicacy for breakfast or even as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Besan
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 tbsp Suji
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • to taste Salt

Method

  1. First, we need to prepare the dhokla batter. Add besan, turmeric powder, oil, yogurt, sugar, salt and water in a bowl. Combine everything together and keep it aside for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Now, add baking soda to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Pour the batter into a steamer and let it steam for 15-20 mins.
  4. Next, we need to prepare the tempering. For this, heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and allow them to splutter. Add curry leaves, green chillies and asafoetida, sugar and water. Close the lid and switch off the flame.

Beetroot Idli

Idli is a popular south-Indian recipe and most of us like to include it in our breakfast. While you must be familiar with all the classic idli recipes, here we bring you a healthy and nutritious beetroot idli that is a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Light roast semolina
  • 1 cup Curd
  • to taste Salt
  • 1/2 cup Beetroot puree
  • 1/2 inch Ginger
  • 3 Green chillies
  • 1 tbsp Cashew nuts

Method

  1. First of all, cut the beetroot and take it in a jar, add green chilies and ginger to it, mix a little water and make a smooth puree.
  2. Put semolina, curd, salt and beetroot puree in a bowl and prepare a batter by adding little water. Keep it aside for some time
  3. Now, take a tempering pan and heat some oil in it. Prepare the tempering by adding mustard seeds, urad dal, finely chopped onions and curry leaves.
  4. Add this tempering to the batter and mix well.
  5. Add eno fruit salt to it and prepare the idli maker.

Salsa Potatoes

Once you make this recipe, we are sure you will keep coming back to it. It is simple to make not only for your family members but also when guests arrive.

Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tbsp Lemon

Method

  1. Take potatoes and cut them in small pieces. Pre heat the oven for five minutes at 150 degrees celcius.
  2. Then put in the potatoes in the oven and let them bake till a crisp. You can add some oil over it or bake it without oil.
  3. Till it bakes, take a small bowl and add salt, pepper, red chilli powder and cumin powder and mix.
  4. Then finely chop tomato and onion.
